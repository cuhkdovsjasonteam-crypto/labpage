### Applications for PhD and Postdoc positions
If you are interested in working with us as a PhD student or postdoc, please send an email directly (dineshb [at] eng.ucsd.edu). State briefly why you are interested and attach a CV. No need for a separate cover letter or certificates. **Important**: please insert _"Application PhD"_ or _"Application Postdoc"_ in the subject line.

### Master projects for UCSD students
If you are a Master student at UCSD looking for a Master project, contact me (or any group member) per email or stop by my office. We also have projects for undergraduate studnets.

### Bsc / Master students from elsewhere
If you are interested in pursuing a Master degree at UCSD, see [https://apply.grad.ucsd.edu/home](https://apply.grad.ucsd.edu/home). Sometimes, we take master students or summer interns if we get exceptional applicants (this usually means very good grades and a personal recommendation).
